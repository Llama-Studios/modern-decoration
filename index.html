Getting Started

To get started, you will need a Carpenter's Bookshelf. It is crafted by surrounding a book with 8 wood planks. To use it, place it down and then break it. This will give you the Carpenter's Guidebook. This book tells you all the recipes.

Tiles

Tiles are decorative blocks that come in all 16 colors.

Quilts

Quilts are decorative blocks that come in all 16 colors.

Wax & Candles

Wax is made by smelting honeycomb. It is used to make candles, which are light sources that come in all 16 colors.

Colored Lamps

Colored lamps are decorative blocks that come in all 16 colors. They light up the same brightness as glowstone.

Caged Lamps

Caged lamps are decorative blocks that come in all 16 colors. They light up the same brightness as glowstone.

Caution Tape and Caution Blocks

Caution tape is crafted from 8 paper surrounding a redstone torch. It is used to make caution blocks, decorative blocks that come in all 16 colors.

Gypsum

Gypsum is found in mesas. It is used to make plaster. You can also craft 4 gypsum dust into 2 bonemeal.

Plaster & Trim

Plaster is a decorative block, made by smelting gypsum dust, or from sand and clay. It can be made into trim using a stonecutter. There are also variants with ceiling lights, fans, outlets and light switches.

Wallpaper

Wallpaper is a decorative block that comes in all 16 colors. It is made from plaster, paper and dye.

More Bookshelves

Bookshelves have been added for all the wood types, including the new Nether wood! The new bookshelves DO NOT work for enchanting, as this is impossible on Bedrock Edition.

Radiators and Fans

Just random decorative blocks. They don't do anything.

Tables

Tables are decorative blocks that come in all 8 wood types.

Panels

Panels are decorative blocks that come in all 8 wood types. They are based on trapdoor textures.

Crates

Crates are decorative blocks that come in all 8 wood types.
